Welcome to OB Montessori Center

Operation Brotherhood Montessori Center (OBMC) is a private educational institution that follows the Montessori approach to learning. The school focuses on academic excellence, discipline, and values formation.

Our Mission

OBMC aims to develop independent, responsible, and confident learners. The school provides quality education that nurtures both intellectual and moral growth.

Our Vision

OBMC envisions students who are globally competitive, socially responsible, and guided by strong values and discipline.

Core Values

Discipline

Students are guided to respect rules and responsibilities.

Excellence

The school encourages students to always do their best.

Integrity

Honesty and respect are practiced inside and outside the classroom.
